Bio

Jessica Parry-Williams MCSP, MACP Clinical Specialist Physiotherapist

July 30, 2019 Jess Parry-Williams

Jess qualified as a Physiotherapist in 2001 and has developed a broad range of clinical experience, from working with the general pubic to elite sports athletes. Jess works as an Advanced Physiotherapy Practitioner within the NHS in injury assessment, diagnosis, management and advanced rehabilitation for return to sport or function. Jess has a Masters degree in Manual Therapy and treats a wide range of musculoskeletal conditions.

Since 2017 Jess has been the lead Physiotherapist for Welsh Triathlon senior and youth/junior squads and has worked with Cardiff MET University Rugby teams. Jess has also worked with Welsh Cycling, Weightlifting and Gymnastics whilst working at the Welsh Institute of Sport.

Within the sporting arena Jess has developed an evidenced based interest and delivered physiotherapy in injury prevention, performance optimisation and advanced rehabilitation back to sport.

 

Sporting Achievements

Jess has represented Wales as a senior athlete in Cross Country, 10 km and Half Marathon distances and was former Welsh Marathon and Half Marathon Champion.

In triathlon Jess represented Great Britain Age Group Team at the World Championships and has podiumed at Half Ironman UK Wimbleball.
