Performance

Jessica Parry-Williams MCSP, MACP Clinical Specialist Physiotherapist

July 30, 2019 Jess Parry-Williams

Jess works with a number of athletes across different disciplines to help maximise their performance though injury prevention screening and programming and hands on treatment. She works within multi-disciplinary teams consulting with Coaches, S&C coaches on training programme content and delivery and strategies to minimise injury risk. This enables athletes stay consistent in their training and ultimately improves overall race performance.

Jess provided me with hockey specific rehabilitation exercises which enabled me to return to hockey in time for the Commonwealth Games stronger and fitter than I was prior to my injury. Issy Morris - Welsh Triathlete

"Jess has been fundamental in getting me as well prepared as possible on that start line. Whether it be flushing out my body pre-race, maintaining it in order to train consistently or providing advice and support on potential injury risks. Jess has given me someone to wholeheartedly trust and confidence to push through a small niggle or take time off and recover when needed. Jess has also contributed to my success by carrying out in depth flexibility and strength screening, and creating programmes to improve my range of motion, movement quality and strength. I have seen long standing niggles and recurring injuries resolved through the combination of hands on treatment, flexibility, core stability and strength work which has in turn improved my robustness in sport. Thank you"
